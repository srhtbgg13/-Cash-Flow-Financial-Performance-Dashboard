Cash Flow & Financial Performance Dashboard

I. PROJECT OVERVIEW

This project focuses on analyzing a company’s financial health through a **Business Intelligence dashboard**, with a case study of PT Unilever Indonesia Tbk.
The dashboard is designed to evaluate cash flow performance, profitability, and working capital efficiency, helping to uncover insights that support better business decision-making.
---

II. OBJECTIVES

* Analyze Operating Cash Flow (CFO) performance.
* Evaluate overall financial performance
* Identify trends and patterns in financial data
* Support data-driven decision making
---

III. DATA OVERVIEW 
* Source : Financial Statements of PT Unilever Indonesia Tbk
* Period: 2018 – 2021
* Type: Quarterly financial data

IV. KEY VARIABLES 
* Cash Flow from Operations (CFO)
* Net Income
* Revenue
* Working Capital

V. SUPPORTING VARIABLES
* Accounts Receivable
* Inventory
* Accounts Payable

---

VI. METHODOLOGY
1. **Data Collection**
   Collected financial statements from the company

2. **Data Preparation**
   Cleaned and structured raw financial data

3. **KPI Calculation**
   * Net Margin
   * Cash Conversion Ratio (CCR)

4. **Data Visualization**
   Built interactive dashboards using **Tableau**

---

## 📊 Dashboard Structure

**Page 1: Cash Flow Performance**

* Displays key metrics:
  * CFO
  * Net Margin
  * Cash Conversion Ratio (CCR)
* Evaluates overall financial performance

**Page 2: Cash Flow Driver Analysis**
* Analyzes working capital components:
  * Accounts Receivable
  * Inventory
  * Accounts Payable
* Identifies key drivers affecting cash flow

---

VII. KEY INSIGHTS
* Profit growth does not always translate into strong cash flow
* Cash Conversion Ratio (CCR) is fluctuating, indicating inconsistent efficiency
* Increasing working capital ties up cash in operations
* Rising accounts receivable slows down cash inflow

💡 Overall Insight:
Cash flow performance is more influenced by **working capital management** than profitability alone
